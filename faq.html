FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Bike Clinic offer?

We provide bike tune-ups, part replacements, tire and tube services, maintenance packages, and personalized purchase advice. We also buy and sell bikes.

Do we help with e-bikes?

Our team is well trained to maintaien and help with E-bikes

Can I walk in without booking?

Yes! Walk-ins are welcome at both locations. Average wait time is 15–20 minutes. Booking ahead ensures faster service.

Do you sell bike accessories?

Yes, we sell safety gear and accessories like helmets, locks, lights, and baskets.

How do I book an appointment?

Click the “Book Appointment” button in the top right or go to book.html.

Do you buy used bikes?

Yes. Bring your bike in-store for a quick evaluation. We offer fair pricing based on condition and market value.
